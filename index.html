Star Fox is a renowned Nintendo rail-shooter/space-flight simulation game series created by Shigeru Miyamoto in 1993, starring Fox McCloud and his team of anthropomorphic animal pilots, including Falco, Peppy, and Slippy.

History

Two years have passed since James McCloud disappeared, the former leader of the Star Fox team. Andross, a psychotic scientist from the planet Venom, has built an army and launched an invasion across the Lylat system.

Under orders from General Pepper, Fox McCloud leads the new Star Fox team to stop Andross and restore peace to the Lylat system.

    Pilots

  • Fox McCloud

    Leader of the Star Fox team.

  • Falco Lombardi

    An elite pilot known for his incredible flying skills.

  • Peppy Hare

    Veteran pilot and strategist.

  • Slippy Toad

    Engineer and mechanic of the team.

Games

Star Fox

was released on the Super Nintendo (SNES) on February 21, 1993 in Japan and March 1993 in North America. It sold over 4 million copies worldwide.

Star Fox 2

Released officially in 2017 on the SNES Classic Edition and later added to Nintendo Switch Online.

Star Fox 64

Released in 1997 for Nintendo 64 and became the best selling game of the series with more than 4 million copies sold.

Star Fox Adventures

Released in 2002 for Nintendo GameCube and sold over 1.8 million copies.

Star Fox Assault

Released in 2005 for GameCube and sold around 1 million copies worldwide.

Star Fox Command

Released in 2006 for Nintendo DS and sold around 500,000 copies worldwide.

Star Fox Zero

Released in 2016 for Wii U with estimated sales between 340,000 and 400,000 copies.
